Chester Bennington

1976 - 2017

He left a legacy in the genre of alternative metal, bringing it to the forefront and affecting the lives of millions.

Here is a list of some of the accomplishments that Chester Bennington achieved during his time as the lead vocalist for Linkin Park:

  • Linkin Park's debut album, Hybrid Theory, was a worldwide commercial success. Bennington's talent as a vocalist began to be noticed. The album was certified Diamond by the Recording Industry Association of America, meaning that it was the best-selling debut album of the decade.
  • Linkin Park has earned several prominent accolades, including two Grammy Awards, six American Music Awards, four MTV Video Music Awards, and three World Music Awards.
  • Billboard ranked Linkin Park #19 on the Best Artists of the Decade chart.
